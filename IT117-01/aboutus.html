35 years ago my grandfather come up with the idea to make the best pizza in town and he not only focused on pizza but he also wanted to make this restaurant a fun place to visit where people can come to have lunch or have dinner with their kids
My grandfather Hamed always had a dream to grow up and start his own business and serve the community at the same time. So on his first day of open he gave out free pizza to anyone who couldn't pay for it. He also donate his first month of profit to support the local School. To this day DeepCrust does a lot of work for its community and this planet.
